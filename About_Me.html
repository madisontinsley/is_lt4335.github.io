My Resume

My name is Madison Tinsley, and I am 21 years old. I grew up in Lee’s Summit, Missouri, about 15 minutes from Kansas City. I lived with my parents and our dog, Marley. Before I started high school, my older half-brother moved in with my parents and me. I joined theater and choir in high school, developing a passion for singing. I decided to pursue a music degree. I graduated from Lee’s Summit North High School in 2020. I went on to attend Pepperdine University in California to study Vocal Performance. I spent my first year online due to the pandemic until my sophomore year in 2021. After a difficult academic year at Pepperdine, I decided to quit music and transfer to the University of Missouri. I began my first semester in the fall of 2022 as a Communications major with an Information Technology (IT) minor, expected to graduate in December of 2024. I am working to complete certificates in IT, and I currently hold a student position in the Division of IT’s Adaptive Computing Technology center at Mizzou. I now plan to establish a career in technology after graduation.
